Staging recommendations
🪟
Open up the window
Reposition the sectional along the wall — restores the bay window as focal point and adds 6+ feet of visual breathing room.
🖼
Depersonalize surfaces
Remove all family photos. Replace with 1–2 large neutral prints or a statement mirror to reflect light into the room.
💡
Swap the brass fixtures
Replace dated floor lamp with a matte black arc lamp ($80–$120 at IKEA). Instant modern update within budget.
🏡
Reveal the hardwood
Pull back the shag carpet to expose hardwood floors. Add a large neutral jute rug ($150–$200) to anchor the seating area.
